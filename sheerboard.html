Winner of Katrinebjerg Kompetition 2014

Sheerboard

My team developed the concept for a design competition during a weekend, January 2014. The case was given by Ditmer and after winning the competition and presenting the concept for the audience and jury, my team were invited to present the concept for the rest of the Ditmer company.

The concept was about making the organizational structure in a small or medium-sized company clear and giving individual users an overview of their company's structure, while being simple and easy to use.
Our solution was the Sheerboard-concept, an automated and interactive visualization tool.

The jury's pronouncement:
"Vi blev i dommerpanelet ret hurtigt enige om, hvilken løsning der skulle vinde. Det er faktisk sejt at kunne holde fast i det enkle i en løsning, som vinderholdet har gjort det. Et enkelt design betyder, at løsningen er nem at eksekvere og dermed nem at brede ud til mange, sagde direktør Morten Ditmer i sin tale til vinderne."

For more information about the competition, see this article by PROSA.
