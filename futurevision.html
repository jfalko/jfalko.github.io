FutureVision

Future Vision was a concept developed for Århus Ø in an Advanced Interaction Design course at the university. An Augmented Reality goggle would show information about buildings and construction sites on Århus Ø, when the user looked at them through the goggles.

To visualize the concept we recorded a video prototype in a blue screen studio, to make it look like the goggles were placed on Århus Ø.

I build the scenary in Unity using 3D models of Århus city. A video about the concept can be seen here.
