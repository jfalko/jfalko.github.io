Jeanette Falk Olesen - Digital Designer Redesign

With my background in Digital Design I have experience in varied fields of interaction design, such as game design, level design, user experience, design processes, concept development, digital aesthetics and critical design.
I work in different materials, such as paper, code, 3D graphics, electronics etc. I'm at my best, when I have to generate ideas, work in a team, work within creative constraints, prototype and work hands on with the design.

My perspective on game design is based on the understanding and appreciation of the player's interaction with the game; how is the game feel for the player?

In my spare time, I help arrange the weekly game collective GOLD. Attendee at Game Jams, whenever time allows.
