Coverbook

In a Social Media course I had at Aarhus University 2015, we had to develop a social media concept for the new Main Library, Dokk1, in Aarhus.

By using the inbuilt RFID chip in the books, the user would log in to a 'social media' site, as the book he/she just scanned. This was to create an experience for the user, by building a narrative around the physical books via a fictitious social media site.

Comment from teacher:
"Først og fremmest: Coverbook-ideen er meget original og supergodt beskrevet! Det er et interessant take på de constraints, som I blevet sat overfor."
